<p>LibreCuts is a free and open-source video editor for Android focused on simplicity, performance, and privacy. Edit and export watermark free videos entirely on your device.</p>

<p><b>Note:</b> LibreCuts is currently in beta. You may encounter bugs, instability, or incomplete features as development continues.</p>

<p><b>Features</b></p>

<ul>
<li><b>Trim:</b> Remove unwanted parts from the beginning or end of a video.</li>
<li><b>Merge:</b> Combine multiple video clips and rearrange them.</li>
<li><b>Overlays:</b> Add text, images, GIFs, and video overlays.</li>
<li><b>Subtitles:</b> Import .srt subtitle files and reposition captions in the preview.</li>
<li><b>Layer Management:</b> Reorder overlay layers.</li>
<li><b>Audio:</b> Import audio, boost volume up to 200%, or mute the original audio.</li>
<li><b>Crop:</b> Crop videos to custom aspect ratios.</li>
<li><b>Transitions:</b> Add smooth transitions between clips.</li>
<li><b>Speed:</b> Speed up or slow down videos.</li>
<li><b>Adjust &amp; Filters:</b> Adjust brightness, contrast, saturation, and apply filters.</li>
<li><b>Reverse:</b> Play videos in reverse.</li>
<li><b>Timeline Tools:</b> Snap clips, duplicate overlays, and create freeze frames.</li>
<li><b>Hardware Acceleration:</b> Export videos faster using supported hardware encoders.</li>
</ul>
